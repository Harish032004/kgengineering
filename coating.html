Types of Floor Coating

We provide specialized flooring solutions for demanding industrial environments. Each coating is engineered for maximum durability and performance.

Epoxy Floor Coating

High-performance seamless coating ideal for factories, warehouses, and commercial garages requiring chemical and abrasion resistance.

  • Extreme durability & impact resistance
  • Chemical & stain resistant
  • Seamless & easy to clean surface
  • Customizable colors and finishes

PU (Polyurethane) Coating

Flexible, UV-resistant coating perfect for outdoor areas, rooftops, and environments with temperature fluctuations.

  • Excellent UV & weather resistance
  • High elasticity for thermal movement
  • Resists yellowing over time
  • Ideal for outdoor applications

Anti-Skid Coating

Safety-focused flooring with enhanced traction for wet areas, ramps, walkways, and food processing facilities.

  • Superior slip resistance even when wet
  • Meets OSHA safety standards
  • Durable under heavy foot traffic
  • Available in various textures

ESD / Anti-Static Flooring

Specialized conductive flooring for electronics manufacturing, server rooms, and areas with sensitive equipment.

  • Dissipates static electricity safely
  • Protects sensitive electronic components
  • Reduces fire and explosion risks
  • Meets ESD industry standards

Chemical Resistant Coating

Engineered to withstand aggressive chemicals, acids, and solvents in laboratories, chemical plants, and industrial facilities.

  • Resists acids, alkalis, and solvents
  • Impermeable barrier protection
  • Prevents concrete degradation
  • Easy decontamination

Heavy Duty Industrial Flooring

Ultra-durable flooring systems designed for extreme conditions including heavy machinery, impact, and thermal shock.

  • Withstands extreme mechanical stress
  • Thermal shock resistant
  • High load-bearing capacity
  • Longest service life available

Our Floor Coating Process

Transparent, step-by-step approach ensuring quality results and long-lasting protection

01

Site Inspection & Surface Assessment

Our certified technicians conduct a thorough inspection of your facility to assess floor conditions, identify issues, and determine the optimal coating solution.

  • Moisture content testing
  • Surface hardness evaluation
  • Existing damage assessment
  • Traffic pattern analysis
Time Required: 1-2 hours
02

Surface Preparation & Cleaning

Proper surface preparation is critical for coating adhesion. We use industrial equipment to clean and profile the surface for optimal bonding.

  • Industrial grinding/scarifying
  • Pressure washing & degreasing
  • Surface profiling for adhesion
  • Complete debris removal
Time Required: 4-8 hours
03

Crack Repair & Leveling

All cracks and uneven surfaces are professionally repaired using industrial-grade materials to ensure a smooth, level foundation.

  • Crack routing and cleaning
  • Epoxy injection for structural cracks
  • Self-leveling compounds application
  • Surface smoothing and finishing
Time Required: 3-6 hours
04

Primer Application

A high-performance primer is applied to enhance adhesion, seal the surface, and provide a uniform base for the top coating.

  • Epoxy or urethane primer selection
  • Even application with industrial rollers
  • Proper drying/curing time
  • Adhesion testing before proceeding
Time Required: 2-4 hours + drying
05

Coating Application

The selected coating system is professionally applied using specialized equipment to ensure even coverage and optimal performance.

  • Epoxy, polyurethane, or polyaspartic coating
  • Multiple coats for enhanced durability
  • Anti-slip additives if required
  • UV-resistant topcoat application
Time Required: 6-12 hours + curing
06

Final Inspection & Handover

Our quality assurance team conducts a final inspection and provides maintenance guidelines before handing over the completed project.

  • Comprehensive quality inspection
  • Adhesion and thickness testing
  • Cleaning and site restoration
  • Maintenance guidelines provided
Time Required: 2-3 hours
